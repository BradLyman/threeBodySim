Three Body Gravitation Simulation

Controls

The controlls are simple: Just click the mouse anywhere on the screen to change where the particle starts at!

When you get tired of watching an orbit, or when the particle leaves the screen, just click again to start a new orbit.

Details

This web-app is implemented with JavaScript and an awesome WebGL/creative-coding library called Three.js. The code is available on my github page and seems to be relatively easy to read.

A true three body system is fairly hard to watch while understanding what is going on. So to get a more interesting/fun result this simulation uses two very large bodies (which we will call "planets") and one insignificantly small body (which we will call the "particle"). The two planets will orbit each other without feeling the effects of the particle. But the particle will have all sorts of interesting orbit paths depending on where it starts. On the black screen the blue circles represent the planets and the white line shows the path that the particle follows while orbiting them.
